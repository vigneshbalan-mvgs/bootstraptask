The New York Giants

The New York Giants are a professional American football team based in the New York metropolitan area. The Giants compete in the National Football League (NFL) as a member club of the National Football Conference (NFC) East division. The team plays its home games at MetLife Stadium in East Rutherford, New Jersey, which it shares with the New York Jets.

Football Team Review: New York Giants
New York Giants

The Giants are one of the most successful teams in NFL history, having won four Super Bowl championships (Super Bowl XXI, Super Bowl XXV, Super Bowl XLII, and Super Bowl XLVI). The Giants are also tied with the Chicago Bears for the most NFL championships with eight.

The Giants are led by head coach Brian Daboll. The team's star players include quarterback Daniel Jones, running back Saquon Barkley, wide receiver Kenny Golladay, and defensive end Leonard Williams.

The Giants are scheduled to play their first game of the 2023 NFL season on September 8, 2023 against the Tennessee Titans.
